Given a set of n samples $$x = \{x_1,\ldots,x_n\} $$

1. **Arithmetic mean value**

$$\bar x = {\sum x_i \over N}$$
<!--more-->
2. **Geometric mean**

$$x=^n\sqrt {\prod_{i=1}^N x_i}$$
